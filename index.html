I am a MS/PhD student in Computer Engineering at UIUC. My thesis advisor is Prof. Yuxiong Wang. My research interests focus in the area of computer vision, few-shot learning and meta-learning. During my undergraduate years, I worked on multiple research projects in the Human-Centered Autonomy Lab advised by Prof. Katherine Driggs-Campbell, focusing on predictive modeling and computer vision in autonomous driving. I am passionate about learning and especially fascinated by the high-impact of deep learning in solving real-world problems!
